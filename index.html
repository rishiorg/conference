About CGC Jhanjeri

Established in 2012, CGC Jhanjeri is the Chandigarh Group of Colleges youngest and most modern campus, affiliated with I. K. Gujral Punjab Technical University (IKGPTU) Jalandhar and approved by the AICTE, Bar Council of India (BCI), Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), and other regulatory bodies. Offering a diverse range of advanced undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Management, Commerce, Law, Computer Application, Journalism, Pharmacy, Sciences, and more, the college prioritizes practical-based education.

About the Conference

The "International Conference on Recent Trends in AI, Robotics, IoT, and Scientific Technology" brings together leading researchers, engineers, and practitioners from around the globe to discuss the latest advancements and innovations in these dynamic fields. This conference serves as a platform for the exchange of cutting-edge ideas and developments that are shaping the future of technology and science. Attendees will have the opportunity to explore the latest research, engage in thought-provoking discussions, and network with peers, fostering collaboration and driving progress in AI, robotics, IoT, and related scientific and technological disciplines.
